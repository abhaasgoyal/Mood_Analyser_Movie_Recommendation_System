'Housefull 4' Box office collection day 11 : Akshay Kumar starrer mints excellent business on the second Monday

The Diwali release comedy entertainer 'Housefull 4' is receiving great response from the audiences from the box office. The multi-starrer film is really running strong at the theatres and is earning good business. According to the latest report of BoxOfficeIndia.com, the film saw a super strong second Monday with collections of Rs. 5.50-5.75 crore nett which is an excellent number for the second Monday behind only Shahid Kapoor starre 'Kabir Singh' and Vicky Kaushal starrer 'Uri - The Surgical Strike' for 2019. The Akshay Kumar starrer has easily beaten the second Monday numbers of the 'War' and 'Mission Mangal'.
